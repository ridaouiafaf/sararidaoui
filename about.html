About

Once her studies at the Faculty of Science and Technology were finished, she turned quite naturally to the art of embroidery.
She has always been attracted to design and interior decoration, a whole world of creation was offered to her then; an extraordinary playground allowing her to give free rein to her imagination and let her emotions speak through the threads.

Having grown up in a village around Oued Oum Rabii, nature is an important inspiration in her daily life. Her love for nature and embroidery made her do what she is doing today.

I her works, she always seeks to go through a photograph that presents a beautiful natural scene and tranform it to a piece of embroidery art. She works a lot by mixing various embroidery techniques while continuing to evolve her style by combining pure Moroccan techniques with much more contemporary and free approaches.

For the moment, she mainly has an approach of visual artist but she also work on specific, customed and personalized orders for clients.
